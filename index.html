Ashvagundeshwara Temple

A Sacred Abode in the Ghats

Welcome to the Ashvagundeshwara Temple, a divine sanctuary nestled in the lush greenery of Chikkamagaluru. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this ancient temple offers a serene environment for spiritual seeking and peace. Experience the divinity of the Malnad region.

Temple Images

Surrounded by the natural beauty of the Western Ghats, the Ashvagundeshwara Temple is a perfect destination for devotees and nature lovers alike. The temple architecture reflects the traditional style of the region, providing a cool and calm atmosphere for meditation.
