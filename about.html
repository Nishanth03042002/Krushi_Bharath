Ashvagundeshwara Temple is a revered shrine in the Malnad region. Devotees visit to seek the blessings of Lord Shiva and experience the tranquility of nature. The temple is known for its simple yet elegant architecture and spiritual ambience.

Puja Timings

Morning: 6:00 AM - 12:30 PM

History

The Ashvagundeshwara Temple has a rich history deeply rooted in the local traditions of Chikkamagaluru. It is believed to be centuries old, serving as a spiritual anchor for the villagers of Kudregundi and surrounding areas. The temple has been maintained by the local community, preserving its sanctity and rituals over generations.

How To Reach

The temple is located in Kudregundi village, near Koppa in Chikkamagaluru district. It is accessible by road from Chikkamagaluru town. Enjoy the scenic drive through coffee plantations to reach this divine abode.
